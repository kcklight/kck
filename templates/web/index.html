How to save energy, save money and save the environment?

The longevity of LED lighting equipment is typically much greater than incandescent or fluorescent lighting equipment. For example, a traditional incandescent light bulb would require about 7~8 times more energy than a LED bulb. One of the most direct benefit from using LED equipment is that the energy bill would reduce significantly. Also, as LED equipment have much longer service life than all the other lighting equipment, you can also save money from replacing them. There aren't any toxic elements in the LED lights so they are also easy to be recycled or disposed without negative impact to the environment.
